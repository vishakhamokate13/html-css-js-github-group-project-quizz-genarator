UPSC


UPSC is India’s most prestigious national-level exam for government jobs. It selects officers like IAS, IPS, IFS, and IRS.
Level: National
Posts: IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, etc.
Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview
Focus: General Studies, Current Affairs, Essay, Optional Subject
Career: Top administrative and policy-making roles in India

MPSC


MPSC (Maharashtra Public Service Commission) MPSC conducts exams for Maharashtra state government jobs.
Level: State (Maharashtra)
Posts: PSI, STI, Deputy Collector, Assistant Section Officer
Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview
Focus: Maharashtra GK, Current Affairs, General Studies
Career: Administrative and state-level government positions

NEET


NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) NEET is the medical entrance exam for students who want to become doctors. Level:National
Field:Medical (MBBS, BDS, BAMS, etc.)
Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
Stage:Single written exam
Career: Doctor, Dentist, Medical professional

JEE


JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) JEE is the engineering entrance exam for admission into top engineering colleges like IITs and NITs. Level: National
Field: Engineering (B.Tech / B.E)
Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Stage: JEE Main + JEE Advanced
Career: Engineer, Scientist, Researcher

RAILWAY


Railway Recruitment (RRB Exams) Railway exams are conducted for various technical and non-technical posts in Indian Railways. Level: National
Field: Government Jobs (Railway Sector)
Subjects: Mathematics, Reasoning, General Awareness, Science
Stage: CBT (Computer Based Test), Skill Test (post-wise)
Career: Station Master, Clerk, Technician, Engineer

BANKING


Banking Exams (IBPS, SBI, RBI) Banking exams are for recruitment in public sector banks. Level: National
Field: Banking & Finance
Subjects: Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, English, General Awareness
Stage: Prelims + Mains + Interview
Career: Bank PO, Clerk, Manager, Officer
