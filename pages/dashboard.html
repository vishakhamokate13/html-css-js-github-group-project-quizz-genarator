UPSA

UPSC is India’s most prestigious national-level exam for government jobs. It selects officers like IAS, IPS, IFS, and IRS. Level: National Posts: IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, etc. Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview Focus: General Studies, Current Affairs, Essay, Optional Subject Career: Top administrative and policy-making roles in India

MPSC

MPSC (Maharashtra Public Service Commission) MPSC conducts exams for Maharashtra state government jobs. Level: State (Maharashtra) Posts: PSI, STI, Deputy Collector, Assistant Section Officer Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview Focus: Maharashtra GK, Current Affairs, General Studies Career: Administrative and state-level government positions
