UPSC


UPSC is India’s most prestigious national-level exam for government jobs. It selects officers like IAS, IPS, IFS, and IRS.

Level: National

Posts: IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, etc.

Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview

Focus: General Studies, Current Affairs, Essay, Optional Subject

Career: Top administrative and policy-making roles in India

MPSC


MPSC (Maharashtra Public Service Commission) MPSC conducts exams for Maharashtra state government jobs.

Level: State (Maharashtra)

Posts: Deputy Collector, DYSP, Tehsildar, BDO

Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview

Focus: Maharashtra History & Geography, Polity, Marathi

Career: State-level administration and gazetted government posts

NEET


NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) NEET is the medical entrance exam for students who want to become doctors.

Level: National

Field: Medical (MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS)

Stages: Single Objective Entrance Exam

Focus: Biology (Botany & Zoology), Physics, Chemistry

Career: Doctor, Surgeon, Dentist, or Medical Professional

JEE


JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) JEE is the engineering entrance exam for admission into top engineering colleges like IITs and NITs.

Level: National

Field: Engineering (IITs, NITs, IIITs, GFTIs)

Stages: JEE Main → JEE Advanced

Focus: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry

Career: Software Engineer, Data Scientist, Tech Researcher

RAILWAY


Railway Recruitment (RRB Exams) Railway exams are conducted for various technical and non-technical posts in Indian Railways.

Level: National

Posts: Station Master, Goods Guard, Clerk, Ticket Examiner

Stages: CBT 1 → CBT 2 → Typing/Aptitude Test

Focus: General Awareness, Mathematics, Reasoning

Career: Secure technical and non-technical roles in Indian Railways

BANKING


Banking Exams (IBPS, SBI, RBI) Banking exams are for recruitment in public sector banks.

Level: National

Posts: Probationary Officer (PO), Specialist Officer (SO), Clerk

Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview (for PO)

Focus: Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, English, Banking Awareness

Career: Financial Analyst, Bank Manager, or Operations Officer
